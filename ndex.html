OVERVIEW

The Campaign Manager is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline and optimize campaign operations for Hon. Mercy Rono's Women Representative candidacy in Bomet County. This powerful tool enables coordinators, agents, and administrators to collaborate effectively, track progress, and manage campaign activities with precision.

Built with modern technology and user-centric design, the platform ensures every team member has the tools they need to contribute to a successful campaign.

GETTING STARTED

1

Receive Login Credentials

The system administrator will provide you with temporary login credentials via secure communication channels. These credentials include:

Username: [provided by admin]
Temporary Password: [provided by admin]
2

First Login & Password Change

Upon your first login, you will be prompted to change your temporary password. Choose a strong, unique password that includes:

  • At least 8 characters
  • Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Numbers and special characters
3

Update Your Profile

Navigate to the Profile tab and complete your profile by:

  • Uploading a clear profile picture
  • Verifying your contact information
  • Adding your role details and location
4

Enable Required Permissions

For full functionality, the app requires two essential permissions:

Notifications

Receive real-time updates on campaign activities and events

Location

Track field activities and optimize territorial coverage

USER ROLES

Administrator

Full system access with the ability to manage users, configure settings, and oversee all campaign operations.

  • ✓ Create and manage user accounts
  • ✓ Access all data and reports
  • ✓ Configure system settings

Coordinator

Manage regional teams, coordinate activities, and track progress across designated areas within Bomet County.

  • ✓ Manage team members
  • ✓ Schedule and track events
  • ✓ View regional reports

Agent

Ground-level campaign workers who collect data, engage with voters, and report field activities.

  • ✓ Input field data
  • ✓ Track personal activities
  • ✓ View assigned tasks

Other Roles

Additional specialized roles can be configured by administrators to support specific campaign functions.

  • ✓ Custom permissions
  • ✓ Tailored access levels
  • ✓ Flexible configurations
