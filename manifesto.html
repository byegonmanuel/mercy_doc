In The Media

Defending the Agenda

Key responses for Radio & TV Interviews.

How does your agenda align with UDA's Bottom-Up approach?

Bottom-Up is about empowering ordinary people. My agenda focuses on women, youth, farmers, MSMEs, and skills development. That is exactly where economic transformation begins—at the grassroots.

Why focus so much on skills instead of handouts?

Handouts end. Skills create lifelong income. BETA is clear: empower people to earn, not depend. Skills and enterprise support make our people productive.

Where does agriculture fit in your agenda?

In Bomet, women are farmers. By supporting women empowerment, cooperatives, value addition, and climate-smart agriculture, we strengthen food security and household incomes.

How does your international experience help Bomet?

It allows me to connect Bomet youth and professionals to global opportunities, investments, and skills—bringing solutions home, not politics.

Why should UDA support your candidature?

Because my agenda does not compete with BETA—it implements it. I translate national policy into real benefits for ordinary people.
